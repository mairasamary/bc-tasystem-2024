Official instructions & forms

All newly hired student employees who will be working for the first time at BC must complete onboarding before they begin to work. Paper forms (I-9, Required Onboarding Form, Payroll Form Statement) are completed in person at the Office of Student Services. Bring completed forms to the Office of Student Services.
